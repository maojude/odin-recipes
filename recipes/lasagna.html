Homemade Lasagna

Description

This is a homemade lasagna recipe with a meaty tomato sauce, and a delicious cheesy filling. This dish satisfies everyone in the family!

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Cheeses: Cottage cheese, mozzarella, Parmesan
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings: Dried Parsley, Salit, Black Peppers
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings.
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe.
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.
